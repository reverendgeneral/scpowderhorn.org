Powder Horn

The Powder Horn was long used by outdoorsmen as one of the most necessary tools for existence. The powder horn carried the propellant that allowed him to sustain daily life.

The powder horn ranged from the very plain, yet functional, to the ornate work of art, but its use was the same: a Vessel to sustain.

The recipient of the Powder Horn recognition is also a Vessel ... to help sustain the spirit of adventure and excitement of the “old” outdoorsman in our youth. The propellant that we carry is the knowledge to share with them.
